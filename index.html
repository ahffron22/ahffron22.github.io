About Me

I am a recently graduated student from the University of Denver's Coding Boot-Camp.

After years of working jobs with little long term potential and little opportunity to advance, I grew tired of the cycle and wanted something more. I had never felt challenged at any of my previous jobs and always believed I should be doing more. I started researching potential fields for a career change that seemed challenging in a new, interesting way and that also had plenty of room to grow. It took some time to find something that really caught my attention, but eventually I stumbled on web development.

I was instantly intrigued and began diving deeper into the potential of web development. I started watching any free class on Youtube that I could find and only wanted more. After about a month or so of watching free classes and reading on my own, I decided this was something I wanted to pursue full time. Six months later I graduated from the University of Denver's Coding Boot-camp with my certificate and can't wait to get into a new career.

Although I do not have any previous web development job experience, I am an extremely self motivated person and very quick learner. I have never been someone who requires supervision to meet deadlines, and when I'm told something needs to get done, it gets done without being told again.
